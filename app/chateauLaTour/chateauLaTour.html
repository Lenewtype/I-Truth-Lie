Chateau La Tour

Years Active: 1999~01/06/2001
Record Label: Lucifer's Record
Kana: シャトー·ラ·トゥール

Chateau la Tour was formed in 1999 under the Luciffer's Record label. At this time, Yuuga's (Yü-ga at this point) compositional style was very typical of many other contemporary bands, although there was still a unique flair to their music. Their songs used mostly clean vocals, often using doubling effects, and ranged from crazy and chaotic (such as "Smile" and "Schizopherenia") to sad and mournful (such as "I Wish" and "Solitude").They were predominately active in the Kanto region of Japan. Chateau La Tour then disbanded in on January 6th, 2001, just shy of two years of activity. Before their demise, the band released three singles, a full album, and made a V.A. appearance. Like many visual-kei bands have done, their full length album consisted of mostly rereleased material, a handful of new songs, and one track that was reinterpreted for the album. 11 months after their disbandment, Yü-ga, guitarist kiyo, and drummer Ke-to, moved on to Kar'MariA.
